Ready to explore the Land of Morning Calm? Join ACE Charter High School’s unforgettable journey to South Korea in Summer 2028! From the neon streets of Seoul to the tranquil temples of Gyeongju, this immersive cultural experience will be packed with history, food, fashion, and fun.

  • Discover Seoul’s vibrant neighborhoods and K-pop culture

  • Explore DMZ history and Korea’s dynamic past and future
  • Shop in Myeongdong, hike in Bukhansan, and soak in Korean spas
